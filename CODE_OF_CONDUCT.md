# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to fostering a welcoming, inclusive, and respectful community around Shell Menu Runner. All contributors, maintainers, and users should feel safe and valued.

## Our Standards

**Expected Behavior:**

- Be respectful and constructive in all interactions
- Welcome diverse perspectives and experiences
- Assume good faith and benevolent intent
- Give and gracefully accept constructive feedback
- Help and support fellow community members
- Focus on what is best for the community

**Unacceptable Behavior:**

- Harassment, discrimination, or abuse of any kind
- Offensive language or derogatory comments
- Dismissive or condescending tone
- Trolling or intentionally disruptive behavior
- Unwelcome sexual advances or attention
- Private harassment or public shaming

## Enforcement

If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior:

1. Document the incident with details and context
2. Report privately to maintainers at [project-email]
3. Do NOT engage publicly or escalate further
4. Provide as much detail as helpful for investigation

The core team will review reports confidentially and take appropriate action.
